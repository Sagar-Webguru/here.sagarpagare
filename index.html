Who I Am

I'm Sagar Pagare, a BBA-CA graduate from Pune with a background that most analysts don't have — I've actually worked in finance before working with data.

As an Accounting & F&B Controller, I tracked budgets, analyzed cost variances, and built financial reports from scratch. I then built a Python tool to automate PDF report generation from MySQL data — because doing it manually didn't make sense. That's when I realized: I belong in data.

Now I'm focused on data analytics — Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI. I don't just query databases, I understand what the numbers mean for a business. Currently seeking internships and entry-level Data Analyst roles where I can grow fast and deliver real value.
